Create Your First Product

Learn how to set up your first software product in LicenPro. Products are the foundation of your licensing system - they represent the software applications you want to protect with licenses.

Estimated time: 5-10 minutes

What is a Product?

In LicenPro, a Product represents your software application that you want to protect with licensing. Each product has its own set of licenses, releases, and configuration settings.

License Management

Generate and manage licenses specific to this product

Release Tracking

Track software versions and distribute updates

RSA Key Pairs

Secure cryptographic keys for license validation

Analytics

Track activations, usage, and license metrics

Prerequisites

Before creating your first product, make sure you have:

✓
A LicenPro Account

You need to be registered and logged into your LicenPro dashboard. If you haven't registered yet, create an account here.

✓
Product Information Ready

Have your product name, description, and basic details ready. You can always edit these later.

Step-by-Step Guide

1

Navigate to Products

Access the products section from your dashboard

From your dashboard, click on "Products" in the left sidebar menu. This will take you to the products list page where you can see all your existing products.

2

Click "Create Product"

Start the product creation process

Click the "Create Product" button in the top right corner of the products page. This will open the product creation form.

3

Enter Product Details

Fill in the required information

  • Product Name: A unique name for your software (e.g., "MyApp Pro")
  • Description: A brief description of what your software does
  • Company Name: Your company or organization name
  • Website URL: (Optional) Your product's website
4

Configure License Settings

Set up your default licensing preferences

  • Default License Type: Choose Standard, Trial, Subscription, etc.
  • Max Activations: How many devices can use one license
  • Heartbeat Interval: How often the app should check license validity
5

Generate RSA Keys

Create encryption keys for license security

Click "Generate RSA Keys" to create a new key pair for your product. These keys are used to sign and verify licenses.

Important: Download and securely store your private key - you'll need it for offline license validation.
6

Save Your Product

Complete the product creation

Review all the information and click "Create Product" to save. Your product is now ready for license generation!

Understanding Product Settings

Here's a detailed explanation of each product setting and how it affects your licensing:

Product Name

The display name of your software product. This appears in license files and the dashboard.

Max Activations

The maximum number of devices that can activate a single license. Set to 1 for single-device licenses.

Heartbeat Interval

How often (in minutes) your application should contact the server to verify the license is still valid.

RSA Key Pair

Cryptographic keys used to sign licenses. The public key is embedded in your app for verification.

Best Practices

Use Descriptive Names: Choose product names that clearly identify your software. This makes it easier to manage multiple products.
Secure Your Keys: Always download and securely backup your RSA private key. If lost, you'll need to regenerate keys and reissue all licenses.
Never Share Private Keys: Your private key should never be shared or embedded in your application. Only the public key should be distributed.
