Create Your First Product

Learn how to set up your first software product in LicenPro. Products represent the applications you want to protect and manage with licenses.

Estimated time: 5-10 minutes

This guide covers the initial dashboard setup and RSA key generation required for your first product.

Product Capabilities

License Management

Generate and manage keys specific to this application.

Release Tracking

Track software versions and distribute updates.

RSA Signing

Enterprise-grade cryptographic license security.

Step-by-Step Guide

1

Navigate to Products

From your dashboard, click on "Products" in the left sidebar menu. This central hub shows all applications currently managed by your account.

2

Primary Details

Click the "Create Product" button in the top right. You'll need to provide:

  • Product Name: A unique name for your software.
  • Description: A brief summary for your records.
  • Company Name: Your organization name.
3

Configure License Settings

Set the default behavior for this product's licenses:

  • Default Type: Perpetual, Trial, or Subscription.
  • Max Activations: Number of devices allowed per key.
  • Heartbeat: How often the app checks validity online.
4

Generate RSA Security Keys

Click "Generate RSA Keys". This creates the cryptographic pair used to sign your licenses.

Security Recommendation

Your Private Key is kept on the LicenPro server to sign licenses. Your application only needs the Public Key embedded in its code for verification.

5

Save & Initialize

Review your settings and click "Create Product". You'll be redirected to the product dashboard where you can start generating licenses immediately.
