Model Details

The Subscription Licensing model is perfect for SaaS-style desktop apps. It requires a periodic "Phoning Home" check (configurable grace periods) to ensure the customer's subscription is still active.

Grace Periods

Configure how many days a subscription remains valid *offline* before requiring a successful server check. This keeps UX smooth during travel or outages.
