Generate Your First License

Once your product is set up, you can generate license keys to distribute to your customers. Licenses are the core of LicenPro's entitlement system.

License Format

By default, LicenPro generates keys in the LP-XXXX-XXXX format. These are human-readable and easy for customers to copy-paste.

Generation Process

1

Select Product

Go to the Products list or a specific product's dashboard and click "Create License".

2

Configure Entitlements

Define what the license is allowed to do:

  • Expiry Date: When the license should stop working (leave blank for Perpetual).
  • Allocated Seats: How many concurrent activations are allowed.
  • Feature Flags: (Optional) Enable specific modules within your application.
3

Issue & Download

Confirm the details and click "Save License". You can then copy the key or download the .lic file for offline distribution.

Offline licenses require the user to import a binary file into your application. If your app is always online, the License Key alone is sufficient to activate.
